Frequently Asked Questions

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. The AI model (RMBG-1.4) runs entirely in your browser using WebAssembly + WebGPU. Your images never leave your device.

Why is the first run slow?

The RMBG-1.4 model (~45MB) downloads once from Hugging Face CDN and is cached permanently. All future uses are instant with no download.

What format is the output?

Transparent PNG. If you pick a background color it exports PNG with that color fill. JPEG does not support transparency.

Is there a limit?

No. Unlimited images and sizes. Only your device RAM is the constraint. WebGPU users get 5–10x faster processing.
